ABOUT US

We are storytellers, dreamers, and cinema lovers at KUET.

Stop just watching the credits—start being in them. Whether you’re an aspiring director with a smartphone or a tech-geek obsessed with lighting, the KUET Film Society is your playground.

We provide the gear, the crew, and the platform; you just bring the vision. From secret screenings to hands-on workshops, join us and turn your "someday" project into this year’s campus premiere.
